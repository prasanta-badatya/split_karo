Split Karo — Free Expense Splitter for Roommates & Bachelors in India

Split rent, ration, vegetable, and electricity bills fairly with your flatmates — equally or day-wise. Free, no sign-up, works offline, and settles up over UPI.

What you can do

  • Split shared expenses among roommates, friends, or trip-mates
  • Choose equal or day-wise splitting
  • Track who owes whom and settle up instantly with UPI
  • Plan group trips and split travel costs

How it works

  1. Create a group and add your roommates
  2. Add expenses like rent, ration, or vegetables as they happen
  3. Split equally or day-wise — Split Karo does the maths
  4. See balances and settle up over UPI

Both. Split equally, or day-wise so someone who stayed fewer days pays less.

Yes — it's an installable PWA that works offline, with your data stored privately on your device.
